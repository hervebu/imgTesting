RWANDA NZIZA
Branding Rwanda to the world, together.
Rwanda Nziza is a cross-country journey designed and implemented by Image Rwanda. It aims at discovering, capturing and divulging the various unknown beautiful sceneries of the Country of a Thousand Hills. This page offers you access to a limited edition of the first-ever release of photographs taken by our team since the project launch a few months ago.

By purchasing your favourite, you not only gain all rights on the same, get to keep that beauty right in your place, but most especially you support the project for it to go on and reach every undiscovered corner of the country. Returns from your purchases will be used to cover project expenses such as transport, accommodation and other logistical requirements.

We travel Rwanda Nziza, will you too?
